Cockapoo

The lucky result of an accidental breeding of a Cocker Spaniel and a Poodle

  • Origin: United States of America
  • Lifespan: 10-12 years
  • Height: 10-15 inches
  • Weight: 6-20 lb
  • Habitat: By the fire or on the bed

As a hybrid breed, the Cockapoo is usually healthy and active. They are exceptionally intelligent, easy to train and is such an amiable dog that they make good therapy dogs for adults and children. Although they have a silky and odourless coat, it does require daily brushing to prevent tangles.
